Cash Flow Chart

The Cash Flow Chart will display the Analysis Case Cash Flow data.

  • You can choose whether to display the data on a Periodic or Cumulative basis, and Raw or Discounted (Present Value).
  • The Source option defines at what level (Roll Up) in the Analysis Case you want to Chart - Analysis Case, Project Set, Jurisdiction, Project or Asset.
    • See Chart Options for an explanation of how to Apply Focus to reduce the number of entries being charted.
  • Each of the elements that make up the Cash Flow are listed as potential entries to be charted.
    • Individual elements or Aggregates (i.e, CAPEX All or CAPEX Drill_Complete)
    • Cash Flow Tags (After Tax, Before Tax, Net Net Revenue or Net Revenue).
    • Taxes and Burdens, Depreciation, etc.

You don't need to re-run the Analysis Case to chart different data, simply toggle what you wish to view. The Data Source page will update with a Tabular presentation of the data currently being charted, which can be Exported to Excel if required.
