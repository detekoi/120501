Types & Definitions

Many reports refer to different Types and Definitions in the Budget report parameters. There are two "Types" and five "Definitions". This page outlines what they mean.

 

Types:

There are Types you will be asked for Capital and Production. A Type refers to the stage in the budget cycle the numbers come from. Available choices are: Forecast, Field Estimates, Actuals, Financial Actuals  and Blended - which will give you a line for each of the values that are already filled in.

 

Definitions:

Definitions determine what weighting and risk factors are applied to the numbers being pulled. There is a different list for every category. The available categories are: Capital, Revenue, Reserve, Production and OPEX.

 

For Capital:

  • Risked Gross: Gross * Probability %
  • Risked Net: Gross * Probability % * WI%
  • Unrisked Gross: Gross Amount
  • Unrisked Net: Gross Amount * WI%

 

For Reserves:

  • Gross Unrisked: Gross Amount
  • Gross Risked: Gross Amount * Probability %
  • Net Risked: Gross Amount * Probability * WI%
  • Net Risked After Royalty: Gross Amount * Probability * WI%
  • Net Unrisked: Gross Amount * WI%
  • Net Unrisked After Royalty: Gross Amount * WI% * Royalty

 

For Production:

  • Gross Unrisked Raw: [Gross * Downtime %] - OPEX
  • Net Unrisked After Royalty: [(Gross * Downtime %) * Probability * WI% * Shrinkage * Royalty] - OPEX
  • Net Risked Raw: [(Gross * Downtime %) * Probability % * WI%] - OPEX
  • Net Risked Sales: [(Gross * Downtime %) * Probability % * WI% * Shrinkage] - OPEX
  • Net Unrisked Sales: [(Gross * Downtime) * WI% * Shrinkage] - OPEX

 

For Revenue:

  • Gross Unrisked Raw: (Gross Unrisked Raw Production) * Price
  • Net Risked After Royalty: (Gross Risked After Royalty Production) * Price
  • Net Risked Raw: (Net Risked Raw Production) * Price
  • Net Risked Sales: (Net Risked Sales Production) * Price
  • Net Unrisked Sales: (Net Unrisked Sales) * Price

 

For OPEX:

  • Gross Unrised Raw: [(Gross Unrisked Raw Production) * Variable OPEX] + Fixed OPEX
  • Net Risked After Royalty: [(Gross Risked After Royalty Production) * Variable OPEX] + Fixed OPEX
  • Net Risked Raw: [(Net Risked Raw Production) * Variable OPEX] + Fixed OPEX
  • Net Risked Sales: [(Net Risked Sales Production) * Variable OPEX] + Fixed OPEX
  • Net Unrisked Sales: [(Net Unrisked Sales) * Variable OPEX] + Fixed OPEX
