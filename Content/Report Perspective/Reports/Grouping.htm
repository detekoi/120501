Grouping

Grouping specifies sub-totals and data order. Groupings applied to a report will appear in the sub-heading of the report, in the order they are listed.

Groupings in the data appear as divisions in the datasets, with headings breaking apart the subsections.  

Main Group:Primary attribute to group by.

Sub Group 1-4: Additional attributes to group by.

 

Example of a Grouped ’CAPEX Report’, it is Grouped by Area, and then by Project.
