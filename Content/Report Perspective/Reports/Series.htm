Series

The series page lets you define the visibility and titles of each series. Changing the "Title" field changes the series title in the Legend.

The Type is always locked for the first series, but the second series can be graphed as any compatible chart style - for example, if your graph type is a Bar Chart, the first series must be a graph, but the second series can be a Bar, Line, Area, Stock, Tube, Pyramid, or Cone.

Stacked and Visible allow you to change the way the data is presented visually.

The Series Palette lets you change the colours of different series.
