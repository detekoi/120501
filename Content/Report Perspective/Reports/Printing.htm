Printing

Printing a Chart is very similar to printing a report.

  1. Press the "Print" button on the main toolbar.
  2. Pressing this button will open the printer preferences window. Select the printer settings and click "OK" or "print" (depending on your individual printer setup).
  3. The Chart will now print.
  4. To print in other formats, or for advanced printing options, you have to export the Chart first.
  5. **Unlike reports, charts will not automatically resize to the selected paper size. They will print as they appear on-screen. You will need to manually adjust the charting window size (which will adjust the chart size accordingly) to get the desired size of printout.
