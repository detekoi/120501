Cash Flow Report Overview

The Cash Flow Report shows your cash flow over a selected time period. It calculates the Revenue, Operating Expenses, Capital Expenses, and provides a sum of the three as "Cash Flow".

This report provides values at the Project level. It gives you a value for each period, as well as an Average over the periods selected and a Total all the periods combined. A Grand Total for each period is available as well as the Average and Totals fields.
