Assigning Series

Selecting  will open the following screens.  Before a chart will plot, you must assign a value to the X and Y axis series. The table at the bottom of the screen contains the available data sets as defined in the "Data Sets" table and the "Dates" screen.

Assign a series by selecting the data set from the bottom window and click-and-dragging it to one of the series fields. The series fields are the boxes identified by the symbol to their immediate right. The images below demonstrate how to assign "Period" to the Y axis.
