CAPEX Report Overview

The CAPEX report shows the breakdown of Capital Expenditures over a selected time period. The report shows each Project in the selected budget, broken down into the respective Assets, and further divided to show the individual Expenditure lines for that asset.

The totals show a sum of the Expenditures at the Asset and Project roll-up levels.

You can customize this report to show different Capital Definitions (Risked, Unrisked, Gross, Net, etc.), different Capital Types (Forecast, Field Estimate, Actuals...). You can only show one combination at a time on this report however, if you want to see a comparison between two, you will need to run the Capital Expenditure Variance report.
