Product Production Report Overview

This report shows your production by Asset and Product over the selected time periods. This report is unique in that it does not force you to select an equivalency to report multiple products - you can select up to 3 products and have the numbers reflect different units for each.

The report gives you the Production numbers over the selected periods for each product you defined, the Onstream date of that product for each Asset, the UWI, the Asset name and the Project  Name.

There is also a total of all the periods combined, and an average. The report includes a grand total that rolls up all the values for each period at the bottom of the report.
