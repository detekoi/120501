Production Report Overview

This report shows your production by Asset and Product over the selected time periods.

The report gives you the Production numbers over the selected periods for each product you defined, the Onstream date of that product for each Asset, the UWI, the Asset name and the Project  Name.

There is also a total of all the periods combined, and an average. The report includes a grand total that rolls up all the values for each period at the bottom of the report.

 

Note on this Report:

Using the Daily Rate toggle

Some users may wish to see the daily production rate, but over a monthly time period. To do this - keep your time period set at "Monthly" and under "Toggles", set "Daily Rate" to on. This will provide you with a report showing the average daily production rate for each month, over the 12-month period defined. The difference between selecting "Daily" as your time period, is if you do that you will only see 12 days worth of production, with the production values day-by-day.
