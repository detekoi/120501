Match Forecast Totals & Date vs. Data Driven Blending

There are two toggles on a number of reports that require special explanation.

 

  • Match Forecast CAPEX Total: distributes extra Forecast CAPEX that was not spent on actuals.  As a result a CAPEX report covering both blended and forecast capital types will match over the entire period of the expenditure.

vs.

  • Use Original CAPEX Forecast: Adjusts the remaining months of forecast data to align with the original Forecast Total

 

Date Based Blending:

  • Actuals are used to a given date, usually a month end  i.e. to Mar 31, 2013
  • Forecast numbers are used for the remaining time period i.e. to Dec 31, 2013

vs.

Data Based Blending:

  • Numbers are blended down to the day based on the cross-reference between AFEs, Expenditures, and Expenditure detail tables.
