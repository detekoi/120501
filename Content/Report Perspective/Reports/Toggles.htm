Toggles

Toggles represent specific options available to each Report.  The options that are available in the Toggles Parameter will vary from Report to Report, however a few Toggles such as ‘Suppress Details’, ‘Exclude Zero and Null’, and ‘Show Chart’ are common.  When a Toggle has a check-mark it is active.
