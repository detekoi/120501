Dates

The dates portion of the Report Parameters define the dates shown on the report.

Reporting Period: Time period the distribution will be based on Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly and Annually choices that are available and depending on the Report Category.    AFE Reports Generally use an inception to date method and do not require Start and End Dates, and Custom Charts allow the user to define the total number of periods to be reported on.

From Date: Starting Date of the reportable data.

To Date:End Date of the reportable date.

The From Date and To Date control the time aperture of a Report. Data that is recorded prior to the From Date or After the End date, with some exceptions, is generally not shown on the report.  Exceptions include the ‘Long Term Reports’ and Site Specific Reports such as the ‘Follow Up Report’ both of which can show data pre and post From and To Dates.

The option to Use Fiscal Calendar works in conjunction with the Fiscal Year option in the Configuration. This toggle changes the column labels (for Quarterly and Annual Reporting Periods) based on the Fiscal Start month that you set.
