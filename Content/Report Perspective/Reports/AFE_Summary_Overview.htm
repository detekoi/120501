AFE Summary Report Overview

The AFE Summary Report shows a detailed breakdown of AFEs. It shows the AFEs, the Approval Date, the AFE amount, and compares it against the Current Forecast, ProRated Forecast, Field Estimates, and Actuals to Date, then creates a Variance heading to show the difference from target.

Finally, it lists whether or not the AFE has gone over the expected amount, what percent has been spent so far, and what the AFE status is (Approved, Rejected, etc.)

At the bottom it also has a Grand Total summarizing the values of the other columns.
