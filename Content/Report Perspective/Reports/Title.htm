Title

 

The Title screen lets you manually define the appearance of the Title bar. The basic options are to change the Chart Title, and title Font.

Click "Text" to open an advanced window for the Title font:

Through here, you can define the relative location on the page, the text wrapping, background colour, if there is an outline for the title, and the orientation in relation to the data, top, and sides of the chart.

Click "Layout" to open an advanced window for the Title Layout:

 

From here, you can set the background colour, if there is a shadow from the text, if there is an outline, and the relative orientation.
