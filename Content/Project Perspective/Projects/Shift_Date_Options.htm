Shift Date Options

The Date Shifting Options icon is shown above.  You can access the Date Shifting Options dialogue from the Date Shifting dialogue: click the "Options" button to open the window below.

 

The Options let you pin, or 'lock' components of the Project or Asset you are shifting. If this option is checked for a given section (such as Production), none of the dates or milestones listed in the Production editor will be shifted despite the other dates changing.

Note: you cannot use pinning an item to break business rules related to expenditure dependencies.

There is also a second location for entering CAPEX and OPEX Inflation percentages on this screen.
