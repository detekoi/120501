Spark Line

The Spark Line allows you to visualize the Profile shown in the Matrix, either Spending or Production.

 

  1. The Grey portion of the window indicates the 'shape' of the selected Data Type, for the highlighted Project.
  2. You can click and drag the Yellow highlight bar to drag the focus period Start Date (note that a little movement can result in a significant change in the Period).
  3. The arrow keys on either side of the line let you scroll further back and forward through time than may be on screen at a given time.
