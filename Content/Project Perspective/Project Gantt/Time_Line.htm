Time Line

The Time Line spans the top of the Gantt Schedule view. This can be used to scroll between dates and adjust the time series being viewed.

To move the date, click, hold, and drag the date to the right (move back in time) or left (move forward).

The yellow date flags appear at today’s date, and the date that the cursor is on.

The section of the time line that your cursor is on will also highlight blue down the Gantt.
