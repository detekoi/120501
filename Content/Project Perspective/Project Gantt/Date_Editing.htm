Date Editing

Dates can be edited through the Project Editor or through the Gantt Chart.

Forecast dates can be dragged and dropped on the Gantt to shift dates. Click the bar you want to shift, click inside the bar and hold, then drag the bar so that the start of the bar lines up with the new first date.

When moving a Project bar, all of the dates contained under that Project will shift accordingly. Refer to the "Shift Dates" topic for a diagram of the rollup dependencies. You can prevent related dates from moving by using the "pin" options found in the Shift Date Options dialogue. OPEX or CAPEX inflation factors will be directly applied to all dates contained under the shifted rollup, regardless of the direction being moved.

Moving an Expenditure with dependencies will not shift all dependant expenditures and related dates by the same amount.

Any changes made on the Gantt are reflected throughout the application.

 

Date Shifting Rules:

  1. Expenditures, OPEX, Reserves and Production must start after the Expenditure on which they are dependent ends (with no overlap).
  2. Resource allocations must start no earlier than the Expenditure they are attached to.

If you attempt to shift dates in a way that violates these rules, the move will be prevented.
