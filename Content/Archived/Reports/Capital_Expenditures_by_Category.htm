Capital Expenditure by Category Report Overview

The Capital Expenditure by Category report provides a roll up at the project level of the Capital Expenditure totals for either the individual Capital Expenditure Categories, or alternatively, by each Capital Expenditure Type.

For each project, you will get a total CAPEX amount for each of the Categories or Types. You can choose to filter down to a single one for very specific reports, or stick with the default (all of them) for a broader overview.

At the bottom it also has a Grand Total summarizing the values of the other columns.
