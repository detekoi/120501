Baseline Reserves

If Production Baseline has been enabled, the default Reserves calculations are based on the cumulative Production Calculations..  

  • For Total Uplift, the Reserves amount is the Total Cumulative Production for the Uplift Segment, less the Total Cumulative Production for the Uplift segment. If the Offstream Date for the Baseline Production segment is later than the Offstream Date for the Uplift segments, it is possible to have negative Reserves amounts calculated.
