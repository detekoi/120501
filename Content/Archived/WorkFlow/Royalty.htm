Royalty

Royalties are maintained at the Asset level and stored as a time-vector at per-Asset, per-Product level which allows for modelling various Royalty calculations.

  1. The Royalties can be stored for up-to three different Types (scenarios) which can represent different Prices (P10, P50, P90).
  2. These different Royalty Types are available on any Report/Analysis View where Production with Royalties is being asked for.  
  3. You have the ability to COPY and PASTE one row of data, multiple rows of data or the entire Royalty table to or from Excel.  This will enable you to manipulate the calculations outside of esi.manageTM to generate a vector of values that can be pasted back into esi.manageTM.

You can access the Royalty screen by clicking on the Royalty tab at the bottom of the Project Editor.

 

Royalty Types are maintained in the Configuration perspective.
