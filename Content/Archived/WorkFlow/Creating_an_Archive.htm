Archives provide a snap-shot of the Evergreen Forecast at appropriate points in time. Archives can either be:

  • Editable - controlled changes can be made to the Archive's data; i.e., select measures can optionally be locked, and write access to the Archive controlled by security settings.
  • Closed - the archive cannot be modified.

Note: an Editable Archive can be Closed once all necessary updates have been made.

Archives are used for Variance analysis - the Initial Plan or Mid-Year update to the plan are typical Archives that are compared against through the year.
