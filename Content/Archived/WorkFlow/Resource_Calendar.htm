Resource Calendar

In the right hand pane of the Resource Gantt view is the Gantt calendar. This shows the times when specific resources are planned and actually in use.

 

  1. Thinner bars are locked and can not be edited. This could be due to the bar representing a budget that has been locked or a summary of the resource usage.
  2. Thicker bars can be edited and these changes will be reflected in the thinner summary bars.
  3. Hovering the mouse over a bar will pop-up a dialogue describing what the Start Date and End Dates are, the total duration (in days) and the number of Resources allocated during that time.
  4. If there is a conflict, an over-committal or overlap of Resources, when you hover the mouse over the bar a pop-up will state: e.g."Schedule Conflict" and the "Allocated" field will be replaced with "Over-Committed".
  5. You can drag-and-drop any editable bars to shift the dates. By clicking the right end of the bar you can increase or decrease the duration and adjust the End date.
