Add Reserve

Reserves are tracked in the Reserve screen.

 

Multiple products can be tracked for a single asset in the same table, but they must be entered as separate reserves.

 

To Add a Reserve

  1. Right click anywhere in the Reserves table. This will open a right click menu.
  2. Select "Add Reserve" from the menu. This will create a new blank row in the table.
  3. Required input is Asset, Product and Units.
    • At this point, you can enter the Technical and Economic Reserves Amounts.
    • If you enter a Book Date, the Technical and Economic Reserves will be initialized from the Cumulative Production from matching Production Segments.
    • Matching is based on Asset, Product, Category, Subcategory and Zone.
  4. Click outside the new row to save the changes.

Technical Reserves

The Technical Reserve fields will be initialized from the cumulative Production (Gross) of Matching Production segments, without Risk or Shrinkage applied.

  • Gross Technical  - Gross Unrisked Raw.
  • Net Technical - after WI%.
  • Net Net Technical - after NRI% (if entered), otherwise the same value as Net Technical.

You have the ability to override the amounts in the Technical Reserve fields, but if you change one, you must change them all. The fields are highlighted when they are calculated and white when they have been manually entered.

 

Economic Reserves

The Economic Reserve fields are calculated from the Technical Reserves fields, with the application of Shrinkage (Gross Unrisked Sales)

  • Gross Economic  - Gross Unrisked Sales.
  • Net Economic - after WI%.
  • Net Net Economic - after NRI% (if entered), otherwise the same value as Net Economic.

You have the ability to override the amounts in the Economic Reserves fields:

  1. If you change one, you must change them all.
  2. If you are entering Economic Reserves amounts, you must supply the Shrinkage.
  3. Reserves Reports and Metrics will always use the Economic Reserves amounts, and the supplied Shrinkage is used to report an Unshrunk Reserves amount.

 

The fields are highlighted when they are calculated and white when they have been manually entered.


 

Subsequent changes to a matching Production segment will propagate to all initialized Reserves Amounts (those that have not been manually overridden).

 **Economic Reserves can also be imported from a third party economics tool.

 

Delete Reserve

  1. To delete a reserve, first left click the item you want to delete, so that it becomes highlighted.
  2. Next, right click anywhere in the Reserves table. This will open a right click menu.
  3. Select "Delete Reserve" from the menu. You will either receive a confirmation message or a warning.
  4. You may receive a confirmation to delete the item, select "OK' to do so.
  5. You may also receive a warning that you do not have access to delete the Reserve. You need to have write access to the project in order to do so.
