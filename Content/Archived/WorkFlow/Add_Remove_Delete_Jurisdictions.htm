Add, Remove, and Edit Jurisdictions

 

Global Jurisdictions

Added by pressing the "Add Jurisdiction" button, or selecting that option from the right click menu.

 

Deleted by selecting the jurisdiction to delete and pressing the "Delete Jurisdiction" button, or highlighting the jurisdiction and deleting it from the right click menu.

 

Note: Deleting a Global Jurisdiction Deletes ALL the Local Jurisdictions contained within it.

 

Local Jurisdictions:

Added by first selecting a Global Jurisdiction to create it under, they pressing the "Add Local Jurisdiction" button, or selecting that option from the right click menu.

 

Deleted by selecting the jurisdiction to delete and pressing the "Delete Jurisdiction" button, or highlighting the jurisdiction and deleting it from the right click menu.

 

All jurisdictions are edited by entering the rates into the Tax Schedule table to the right. Clicking a year will expand it so you can enter a monthly value for each.
