Metric Fields

  1. Score: Flag determining if the metric will be included in the score.
  2. Metric: Drop-down populated by the available Metrics. (As defined by the "Budget Metrics" page in the Configuration Perspective.
  3. Unit: The unit the metric is calculated in.
  4. Weight: A relative weighting factor which allows one metric to be weighted more heavily than another for the purposes of calculating the score.
  5. Target: The desired goal of the entire portfolio as it relates to a given measure.
  6. Ceiling: The upper bound of the score calculation. Any projects with higher values than the ceiling will be scored as if they were the ceiling Value.
  7. Floor: The lower bound of the score calculation. Any projects with lower values than the floor will be scored as if they were the floor value.
  8. Order: When including multiple metrics, this determines the order they will appear in the table.
