Summary of Ranked Projects

The Summary table is found in the lower third of the Ranking perspective.

The Summary provides a roll-up of the information in the Ranking Results table. It shows the lowest value in the selected projects, highest value, sum of all values, target values and the difference between the total and target.

The Refresh button (upper right corner of the image below) controls whether the Ranking Summary is updated automatically, or if it is to be update manually (unpressed state).
