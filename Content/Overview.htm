esi.manage Version 3.26

The esi.manageTM  solution provides a real-time overview of your upstream Oil & Gas business with the ability to drill down into detail as needed.

esi.manageTM  lets you plan the following for your budget:

  •   Production
  •   Capital expenditures
  •   Operating expenditures
  •   Reserves
  •   Scheduling

As you implement your budget, esi.manageTM tracks those components, providing real-time variance between the current forecast and the original budget. The availability of accurate, current information allows you to address anomalies in key metrics in a timely fashion. This combination of the forecasting & lookback capabilities with up to date information enables you to react effectively to changing market conditions.

By using esi.manageTM , E&P companies achieve better visibility of the entire production life cycle, including improved accountability, and measure successes and mitigate failures through learnings and better business practices.
