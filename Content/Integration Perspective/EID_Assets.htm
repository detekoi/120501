Searching Assets & EIDs

The Assets screen of the Integration Perspective allows you to search by assets, find External Identifiers, and map the two to each other (and thus their associated production, expenditures, etc.).

You can use either search (Assets or EIDs) by typing in full or partial name matches. For partial matches, you can use %, *  wildcard operators to complete the end of the name. For anything other than a complete match, you will need to check the "Show All" option to see partial matches.

Only Assets from Open or Visible budgets will be accessible in the asset list.

To find assets similar to another asset, right-click on the similar field and select "Find Items Like This". The search criteria defining that characteristic will be added to the search bar. (Eg. +budgetName:"evergreen forecast").  This is a good way to understand the syntax of the search criteria.

The "Smart Filter" applies additional search logic when looking for EIDs, you can also choose to ignore EIDs already mapped to other assets (much like the AFE mapping screen).
