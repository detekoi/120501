Edit External Identifiers

This screen allows you to edit EIDs that you have either already entered or imported through the External Identifier Import.

This list drives the EIDs that will appear in the list when you search for EIDs in the other screens of the Integration perspective.

You can add EIDs by filling out the two bolded mandatory fields.

You can delete EIDs by selecting one from the table and right clicking and selecting "Delete". You cannot delete EIDs that are currently mapped, they must be unmapped before you are able to delete them.
