Integration Perspective Overview

External Systems, for example Field Capture or Accounting systems, will have a unique identifier that represents a certain Well. This may be a Cost Center, or it may be a form of the well's UWI.

To streamline the importing of 'Actuals' data into esi.manage, a mechanism has been provided which allows the mapping of an External System Identifier (EID) to a corresponding esi.manage Asset. An EID is made up of two parts, the System, and the Identifier.

Multiple EIDs can be associated against the same Asset, i.e., one which corresponds to the Field Capture system, and one which corresponds to the Accounting. In addition, a single EID can be mapped against multiple Assets, with a corresponding allocation against Asset.

The Integration Perspective is where the mapping is occurs. EID information can be entered in manually, or can be Imported.
