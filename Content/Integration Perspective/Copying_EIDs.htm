EIDs and Budget Progression

When changing a Budgets Type, EIDs may or may not be copied, depending on the type the budget is changing from/to.

Budget to Current Forecast: EIDs will be copied over, and will still be mapped to the Asset.

Current Forecast to Editable Closed Archive:  EIDs will NOT be copied over into the new Budget.  Users can still manually enter EIDs if they choose to import Actuals into Editable Archives.

Current Forecast to Closed Archive: Any EIDs associated with the Current Forecast will be removed.  

Editable Closed Archive to Closed Archive: Any EIDs associated with the Editable Archive will be removed.
