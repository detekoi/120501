Form View

Some tables in esi.manageTM can also be edited as a form. Clicking the "Form View" button opens a separate window with fields that can be tabbed through, instead of entering the data column-by-column in the table. The form can also be opened by double-clicking on the grey numbered square beside a line in the table or selecting the row and pressing the "F2" button on the keyboard.

 

This is an example of the form for entering Assets. Data is committed (saved) every time you hit one of the arrows (Previous / Next Record) or "OK".
