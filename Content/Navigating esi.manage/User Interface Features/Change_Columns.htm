Change Columns

The tables in esi.manageTM can all be modified to suit your needs. Fields can be hidden from the table, and the order of headings can be rearranged.

 

To Change Columns:

  1. You can access the "Change Columns" functionality by right clicking any of the tables in esi.manageTMand selecting "Change Columns".
  1. Change Columns lets you customize the appearance of the table, so that the most important information is first in the table. Selecting it opens the following screen.

  1. The "Right" and "Left" buttons Add or Remove fields from the display. Available items will not show up in the table, and Selected ones will.
  2. The "Up" and "Down" buttons adjust the position of the selected items in the table. Moving an item "up" will bring it further left in the table, and "down" will move it to the right.
  3. The "Top" and "Bottom" buttons will move the selected Field first or last in the list.
  4. Multiple Fields can be selected and moved simultaneously.
  5. Drag and Drop is enabled with this view.
