Taxes

Taxes in esi.manage can be configured by default in the fiscal model page of the default section within the configuration perspective. Taxes can also be applied to individual projects within the 'Burdens' tab in the project editor.

To configure a tax, navigate to the burdens tab of the respective project and click into the 'Taxes' tab.

 

To configure a new tax, simply click on the notepad icon in the top right of the screen. Click on 'add tax' in the setup wizard, provide a name for the tax and click 'OK'. The tax percentages can then be added into the front of the main taxes screen. The percentage will autofill down the page until a different percentage is added.
