Ownership Behaviour

  • From the Ownership tab you can apply working interest at two levels; the first is at the Project level, and the second is at the Asset level.
  • Working interest can also be applied through the Ownership tab to CAPEX, Production, and OPEX individually or across all the measures equally.
  • The periodicity in the ownership table can be toggled between yearly and monthly for added levels of detail for the time-variant nature of working interest by clicking
  • The default working interest for the project is 100%, if any changes are made, this propagates to all levels below where the change was made (unless manual entries are already made at the lower levels)
  • Any level that has been edited is marked with a small pencil icon.
  • Any line that is in the process of being edited will show an asterisk symbol beside the year being edited.
