Fixed OPEX Fields

Descriptionof Type Options

  • User Supplied – input by the user
  • Selection – a drop down selection, based on choices configured elsewhere. Selections may be filtered based on previously specified or selected data.
  • Default – will be defaulted to a value, but may be overwritten
  • Initialized – will be defaulted to a calculated, or propagated value, but may be overwritten
  • Calculated – will be calculated from other data, and cannot be changed
  • Display – information

Relationships and Impacts

  1. Asset
    • Selection of an Asset establishes a relationship between the Opex and all other Measures (Capex, Production, Reserves).
    • WI% synchronization
  2. Product, and Type
    1. Is the basis for what the operating cost is associated with.
    2. Product and Type are Filter/Grouping options for Reports.
    3. Product is also used in Dollar per Volume calculations (Lifting Cost) to associate the Operating Expense with the corresponding Production.
  3. Status
    • Status is set by User, can be used for filtering in Opex reports.
    • Status options can be defined in the configuration perspective for improved filtering.
  4. Periodicity
    • Determines the granularity of data dispersion.
  5. While Producing
    1. Flag to specify whether Opex is applied only while the well is producing or if it is independent to production.
