Price-Based Opex

 

Price-Based Opex is similar to Variable Opex, except that the schedule and currency are provided by the attached price profile.

In essence, there are cases where a price can be tied to a production stream to account for gas being flared or steam being produced by purchased gas.

For example, a product can be set up for Purchased Gas, and then a production stream created within a project for the amount of gas purchased for producing steam in an injector.

 

A Price-Based Opex can then be attached to that purchased gas to net the revenue to zero for that purchase. This is done by selecting the Purchased Gas product, associating it with and Opex Type, and attaching a Price Profile. The Price profile will assume a default if there is one associated with the product. If there is no default for the selected product, and no price profile selected, the Opex will be reported as 0.
