Asset

In most cases an Asset is a Well, however it could also be a Facility, Base Production for an area, a Cost Center, etc.

Expenditures, Production, Reserve Additions and Operating Expenses are associated against the Well to represent the Activity and the corresponding Outcome.

Multiple Assets can be contained within a single Project. This approach is most appropriate when the Activities are related - for example when multiple wells are to be drilled from the same pad, or when a second drill is dependent on the success of the first.
