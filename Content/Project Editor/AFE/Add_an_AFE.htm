Add an AFE

To add a new AFE

  1. Open a Project and select the AFE tab.
  2. Right click anywhere in the AFEs table and select "Add AFE" from the menu. There is also a button in the top right of the view to do this.
  3. This will create a blank row in the table which you can enter. Fill in the required fields by double-clicking them, and either typing the information in or selecting options from a drop-down list.
  4. To save the changes, click anywhere outside of the newly created row and the changes will be committed to the database.
  5. Repeat as necessary for all AFEs associated with the project.

 (This process applies to AFE Forecasts, Field Estimates, and Actuals also.)

 

Delete an AFE

To delete an AFE Line Item, Forecast, Field Estimate, or Actual:

  1. First select the AFE you wish to delete.
  2. Next, right click to open the menu, and select "Delete AFE". You may also choose to use the Delete AFE button.
  3. After this, you will see a request to confirm the delete - click "OK" to confirm.
