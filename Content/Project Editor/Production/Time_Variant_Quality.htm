Time Variant Quality

Product Quality can be entered over time on the production editor page. This allows for modelling a change in quality of products as conditions in the well change over time.

Click on the Quality tab at the top of the upper table in the Production editor screen.

 

Once the Quality tab is opened, the product quality and quality unit can be entered for each production stream on each asset. The Quality that is entered will have an effect on Pricing and Revenue calculations, as higher qualities tend to have higher price values associated with them.

The table also supports a toggle between monthly and annual periods.
