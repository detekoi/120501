Shrinkage

Shrinkage, along with production volumes and downtime are entered in the production editor screen.

Click on the Shrinkage tab at the top of the upper table in the production editor screen.

 

Once the shrinkage tab is opened, a shrinkage percentage can be added by product for each different asset that has associated production. The table supports right click copy and paste functions, so values can easily be pasted in from excel.

The table also supports a toggle between monthly and annual periods.

The Shrinkage % represents 'losses' which may be due to Derived Product(s), line losses, or consumption for compression. This factor is represented in Production Calculations via the Raw versus Sales Production definition.

Please see 'Derived Products Calculations' for how shrinkage affects derived product calculations.
