Production Pricing

Each Production segment has a Price Profile associated with it to allow Revenue Calculations.

 

 

Default Price Profiles (either from the Global Configuration, Jurisdiction Level, or Project Level) will be shown in yellow.

You have the option of selecting a specific Price Profile for a particular Production Segment, and manually selected Prices appear on a white background

 

See Prices for defining a Price Forecast within the Project, or  Pricing Model for an overview.
