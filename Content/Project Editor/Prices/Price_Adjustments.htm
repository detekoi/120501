Project Level Price Forecasts

In addition to creating Global and Jurisdiction level Price Forecasts, you have the option of creating an adjustment at the Project Level.

This may be for specific Product Quality, Transportation or Capacity considerations.

In this illustration, an adjustment is made to an existing Price Forecast. Note that only root Price Forecasts (WTI, Fort Saskatchewan) as well as Price Forecasts which have been created for this particular Project's Jurisdiction (Saskatchewan) will be available to serve as the Base Forecast.

As described previously, a Price Forecast will inherit the Production Unit and Currency of it's Base.
