Scheduling an Expenditure

When an Expenditures can be scheduled to show a breakdown of spending over a given period of time.

The gold bar icon - "Schedule" will become available once a line item is selected. Click this to launch the scheduling wizard.

The Current Start Date, End Date and Gross Amount are filled in based on the selected Expenditure.

 

There are several ways to adjust the Expenditure:

 

  1. You can modify the Dates for the Expenditure through the Proposed fields.
  2. A Delta Amount (Unscheduled Gross Amount) can be entered, and then applied (see below) to the existing Forecast Scheduled Amounts.
  3. You can Shift the entire Schedule in increments (Weekly, Monthly, etc.).

The Scheduling Dialogue is typically used when you have an Unscheduled Gross Amount (positive or negative) that you want to apply in some manner to an existing Schedule.

  • Distribute will evenly distribute the "Unscheduled Gross Amount" into the Scheduled entries.
  • Fill Up  will create additional entries (based on new Proposed Dates) and add the Unscheduled Gross Amount into those periods.
  • Add In will add the "Unscheduled Gross Amount" into the selected line in the Forecast.
