Gross EAC (Estimated at Completion)

The Estimate at Completion (EAC) fields show the expected final AFE amount based on the current to-date AFE expenditure.

The Gross field can be entered by the user or imported, the Estimated field is calculated.

The Estimate is generated as a projection of the to-date values (taking Field Estimates or Actuals, whichever is available) and compares it against the initial AFE estimate, then carries forward that ratio to the end of the life of the AFE and provides a prediction of the final spend amount.

 

Commit Gross EAC

In the AFE's table at the bottom of the expenditure screen, this button allows you to enter your Gross EAC value as the final amount for the end of the AFE.
