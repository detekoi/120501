Editing Dependencies

To change the order of dependencies, users are able to link, and unlink, measures from each other.  There are several ways to accomplish this, with click and drag being the simplest.

Dependencies can also be linked/unlinked by dragging and dropping the desired dependencies under the desired “Node”. When you click on a measure and begin to drag, it will become transparent, and other measures that you hover over will become highlighted.
