Dependencies Overview

esi.manage includes a single-page mechanism to correlate multiple Measures to one another in Dependent relationships. This is accomplished using the Dependencies page tab under the Project View. The Dependencies page tab allows single page entry of the correlations between Expenditure, Production, Fixed OPEX and Resource Allocation.

In the example below, each well has Drilling, Completion, and Equipment & Tie-in expenditures associated with it, and they are dependent on each other so that an expenditure cannot begin until the previous expenditure is completed.

 

Dependencies are not limited to merely expenditures, nor are they limited to be within a single Asset.  It is possible for the expenditure of Asset 2 to be dependent on the completion of production of Asset 1.  In this way you are able to model Daisy Chain Workovers, where the Workover expenditure will begin after the calculated offstream date of a production stream, and more production will continue after.  See an example below.
