Import PEEP Reports

esi.manage can store reports generated by PEEP in the associated esi.manage project under the attachments tab.  PEEP Reports can be Imported directly by pressing the Import PEEP Reports button.

To do this, the reports have to be set up in esi.manage configuration>system>Miscellaneous>PEEP reports. The precise name of the report as it appears in PEEP has to be entered into the PEEP reports table together with the report currency and whether you would like Nominal or Real data.
