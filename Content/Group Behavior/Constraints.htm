Constraints

Constraints can be used to model behaviours such as the constraints that a facility imposes on a field.  Constraints in esi.manage™ are applied to specific products, so that gas can be constrained differently from oil, and so forth.  Groups can be constrained in two different ways:

  • Capacity – A facility can only process products at certain levels, which can be less than the wells in a field could produce at.  Capacity constraints model the limits a facility places on the production of a field.
  • Downtime – Groups level downtime can be used to model facility downtime and the effects it has on multiple projects at once.

 

To add Constraints to a Product Stream:

  • Open up a group from the Group Browser – it will open to the Group Details tab.
  • Navigate to the Constraints tab.
  • Choose a product to add constraints to and set it to active.
  • New columns will appear under the Annual Constraint Rate by Product, Value table.
  • You can now add a capacity and downtime constraint to the group for the product you have selected by editing the Constraint Table.

 

To Remove Constraints from a Product Stream:

From the Constraints Tab, select the product you no longer want constrained and press the Delete Constraint Product button.
