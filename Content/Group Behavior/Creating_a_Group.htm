Creating a Group

To add a group:

Start by switching to the Groups Tab from the Projects Perspective.

The Groups Tab is found on the right-hand side of the screen, beside the Project List Tab.

  • In the table in the Group Browser, right click and select “Add Group”.  The Add Group screen will appear

 

  • Fill out the Add New Group form.  You will see that each one of the fields is required as they are all listed in bold.
  • Clicking the Finish button will create the group and return you to the Projects Perspective.

  • The next step is to Add Projects to your Group.
  • The last step is to add Production Constraints (curtailment)

 

Deleting a Group

You can delete a group at any time. You will not just delete it from your view, but from the database.  Deleting a Group for you deletes it for everyone.

There are two ways you can delete a Group:

  • Highlight the Group in the Group View, then right click and select “Delete Group”
  • Highlight the Group in the Group View, then use the “Delete Group” button (shown above)
  • A warning message appears confirming you want to delete the selected record.  If you are sure, click “Ok”
