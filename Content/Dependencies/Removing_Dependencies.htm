Removing Dependencies

There are multiple ways to remove dependencies:

  1. Select your measure and click the ‘Unlink’ button .  This will unlink the measure from the associated parent(s).  The effected measure will be moved to the top of the measures list where it will appear with no correlation to any other measures.
  2. Alternatively, a measure can also be deleted directly from the ‘Dependencies’ tab, which will remove it from the linkage, and will also remove itself and all dependent measures.

Note: deleting a measure in the Dependencies tab, will result in that entity being removed from the measures corresponding tab as well, and from the Project entirely.
