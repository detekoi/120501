Revenue Calculations

Forecast Revenue Calculations

Forecast revenue is determined from:

  1. Forecast Production (per Product) in a selected Definition
  2. A Price Scenario
  3. The Price Forecasts within that Scenario
  4. Product Quality Conversion units - if the Price Forecast is expressed in units other than Volume

See Pricing Model

 

Non-Forecast Revenue

Non Forecast (Field Estimates, Actuals, Financial Actuals and Blended) will only use the Revenue amounts that are associated with the corresponding Production Actuals entries. The Revenue will not be estimated by applying a Price File against a Non-Forecast production amount.
