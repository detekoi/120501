Reserve Fields

Description of Type Options

  • User Supplied – input by the user
  • Selection – a drop down selection, based on choices configured elsewhere. Selections may be filtered based on previously specified or selected data.
  • Default – will be defaulted to a value, but may be overwritten
  • Initialized – will be defaulted to a calculated, or propagated value, but may be overwritten
  • Calculated – will be calculated from other data, and cannot be changed
  • Display – information

Relationships and Impacts

  1. Asset
    • Selection of an Asset establishes a relationship between the Reserves and all other Measures (CAPEX, Production, and OPEX).
    • WI% synchronization
    • POS% synchronization
  2. Category, Sub-Category and Zone
    • If Category is the initial selection, the Sub-Category list will be filtered accordingly. If Sub-Category is initially selected, Category will be populated with the corresponding Category.
    • Category, Sub-Category and Zone are matching (to corresponding Production Segments) criteria for the calculation of the Technical Reserves amounts.
  3. Product and Units
    • Product is used a part of the matching criteria to Production Segments.
    • Product and Units define how the Reserves are to be represented.
  4. Raw Technical Amounts (Gross, Net, Net Net)
    • If matching Production segments are found, these values will be calculated.
    • The Gross Amount is Cumulative Production to the Offstream Date/Rate
    • Net and Net Net are calculated from the corresponding cumulative Net and Net Net Production
  5. Raw Economic Amounts (Gross, Net, Net Net)
    • Initially calculated (if matching segments are found).
    • Based on corresponding Shrunk production
    • May be overwritten; if one is overwritten, all must be supplied.
    • If changed, WI% will be calculated based on supplied data – relationship between Gross Econ Sales and Net Econ Sales.
    • Shrinkage can be supplied, which is used for Reporting an Unshrunk reserves amount in Reserves Reports.
  6. WI %
    • Will be initialized from the WI% of the selected Asset.
    • Will be calculated based on supplied values for Gross Econ and Net Econ reserves amounts.
  7. Shrinkage
    • Only supplied if Economics Reserves amount have been provided, in which case the Shrinkage is used to Report an Unshrunk Reserves amount (all Reports are based on the Economics Reserves Amounts).
  8. POS%
    • Can only be supplied if:
    • No matching Production segments are found.
    • No Dependant Expenditure has been selected. If it has been, the NPD% of the Dependant Expenditure is used.
  9. Book Date, Booked
    • Used in Reports.
  10. Dependant On
    • This is the list of Expenditures in the project (regardless of Asset association).
    • The POS% of the Reserves amount will be the calculated NPD% of the Dependant Expenditure.
