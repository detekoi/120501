Capital Depreciation

Calculation:

Depreciation is determined by the Capital Line Items on a per Asset basis.

Default Depreciation is defined in Configuration, but may be over-ridden on a Line Item by Line Item basis.

Description:

Capital Depreciation is aggregated across all Assets in a Jurisdiction when calculating 'Taxable Income' for Government Tax.

Note: Default Capital Depreciation may be configured in the Expenditure Type and Depreciation page of the Configuration.
