Burdens

There are two forms of Burdens, Local Tax and Royalties.

Local Tax

  • This is inherited from the Configuration based on the Project's Jurisdiction, but may be overridden on an Asset by Asset basis.
  • Local Tax% as well as Production or Revenue Taxes can be modified.
  • These tax rates are used in Economics Calculations.

Royalties

  • Royalties can be supplied for each Product/Asset combination within the Project.
  • Three schemes are available.
  • The defined Royalty Rates are used for Forecast Net after Royalty Production and Revenue calculations.
