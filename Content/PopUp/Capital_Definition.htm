Capital Definition

esi.manage recognize data types (monetary) for Expenditures:

  • Forecast - represents what is expected to occur.
  • Field Estimate - the Project Manager's estimate of the actual cost of the activity over a given period, based on progress to date.
  • Actuals - generally what comes from the accounting department in terms of Invoices and Accruals.

A Blended number (for CAPEX) will use Actuals up to a specified date (the Last Actuals Date), and Forecast numbers after that date.

The configuration of this Table is Fixed, the check marks indicate internally what Data Type is being used. The Name of a Capital Type can be changed to reflect your company's terminology. These are the entries that appear in the Capital Type drop down of Reports, Export Jobs, and Analysis Cases.
