AFE(s)

An AFE is mapped against an Expenditure. The allowable mappings are:

  • 1 AFE to 1 Expenditure
  • 1 AFE to n Expenditures
  • n AFEs to 1 Expenditure

When an association is created, the AFE will 'hold' the Field Estimates and Actuals, which will appear as calculated values within the Expenditure.

EAC (Estimate at Completion) can be used to update the Gross Amount for the Expenditure.
