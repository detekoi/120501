There are two types of Price Forecasts available for creation.

The first is a top level Price Forecast which is entered as a single price number.  An example of a top level Price forecast would be your base WTI/Brent price for Oil and NYMEX for Gas.

The second is a nested Price forecast which is entered as a scale and offset against the node above it.  There is no limit as to the number of levels of nesting are used.
