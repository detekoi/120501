Discounting Options

Discounting of the Cash Flow is based on 4 parameters:

  • Economic Discounting As Of - the Date after which discounting will be applied.
  • Annual Discount Rate - the yearly Discount rate (which will be converted to an effective monthly value if Monthly Discounting is chosen).
  • Mid Year - toggle to use Mid Year discounting when there are Periods that have Annual Discounting.
  • Retroactive - for values which occur prior to the Discounting As Of Date, a toggle to inflate values prior to that date.
