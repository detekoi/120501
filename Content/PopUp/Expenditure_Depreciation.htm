Expenditure Depreciation

Depreciation is used in the After Tax Economics calculations. Each Expenditure Type can have a Default Depreciation model configured, on a per Jurisdiction (Government) basis. The model is comprised of:

  • Tangible / Intangible split.

The Tangible and Intangible components are defined by:

  • Useful Life of the 'Asset'
  • Decline Model (Straight Line, Declining Balance, etc) and the Decline Percentage

The results of the Depreciation calculations can be viewed within a Project (and manually over-ridden within the Project).
