Expenditure Dependency

Other Measure (Production, Reserves and OPEX), as well as the Asset, can be made Dependent on an Expenditure.

  • The POS% of the Measure will be calculated as the NPD% of the Expenditure.
  • Updates to the Expenditure NPD% will propagate to the Measures.

In addition, for Production and OPEX:

  • The Start Date for the Measure must be later than the End Date of the Expenditure.
