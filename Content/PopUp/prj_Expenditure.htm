Expenditure

Every Expenditure must be associated against an Asset.

  • The Expenditure is initialized with the WI% of the Asset

Each Expenditure has a Start and End Date, an Amount and a Probability of Doing (PD%).

Expenditures can be 'chained' together:

  • The Start Date of an Expenditure must be later than the End Date of the Expenditure it is Dependent On.
  • PD% are propagated through the chain, resulting in a cumulative Net Probability of Doing (NPD%) for each Expenditure.

Other Measures, as well as the Asset, can be made Dependent on an Expenditure, in which case the NPD% of that Expenditure is calculated into the Probability of Success (POS%) for the Measure.
