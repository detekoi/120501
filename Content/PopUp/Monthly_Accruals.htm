Monthly Accruals

The first pass through the Month End process (particularly for Capital Expenditures) involves:

  • Ensuring that the Field Estimates, represent the best estimate of the work done (in terms of dollar amounts) in the current month.
  • Entering whatever Actuals have been received in the current month.

Part of the current month's Actuals will potentially represent work that was accrued in previous months - so the determination of how much to accrue in the current month will/may be the delta between Field Estimates (for the period of the particular Expenditure) less the Actuals over the same period.

A similar process can be applied for OPEX and Production, particularly for non-operated assets.

Once the accrual amounts have been determined, new Import Files can be generated and imported.
