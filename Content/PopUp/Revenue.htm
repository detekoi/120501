Revenue - Description

All Production Revenue will be read into the Cash Flow on a well-by-well by product basis. The Revenue calculated will depend on the Price File selected in the Analysis Case, as well as additional Price File Adjustments on a per Project basis.

Measure Source:

  • By Asset
  • By Product

Variations:

As defined in Analysis case

May be truncated when Analysis Case Truncation is applied.

Truncation will take place with the Economic Limit of Net Net Revenue.
