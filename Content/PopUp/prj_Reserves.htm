Reserves

Each Reserves Segment must be associated against an Asset.

  • The Reserves Segment is initialized with the WI% and POS% of the Asset

A Reserves Segment can be made Dependent on an Expenditure, in which case:

  •  The POS% is calculated as the NPD% of that Expenditure.

Matching Production Segments; Asset, Product, SubCategory and Zone will be calculated to determine the Total Cumulative Production, which will initialize the Reserves Amounts.
