Production

Each Production Segment must be associated against an Asset.

  • The Production Segment is initialized with the WI% and POS% of the Asset

A Production Segment can be made Dependent on an Expenditure, in which case:

  •  The POS% is calculated as the NPD% of that Expenditure.
  • Updates to that NPD% will propagate to the Production Segment.
  • The OnStream Date for the Segment must be later than the End Date of that Expenditure.
