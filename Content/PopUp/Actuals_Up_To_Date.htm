Actuals Up To Date

Month End is often an iterative process reconciling the different sources of data, i.e.;

  • Field Estimates - which represent the Project Manager's view of the progress, particularly for Capital Expenditures
  • Actuals - which represents what the Finance department has received in terms of invoices.

 For a Project, the delta between Field Estimates to date, and Actuals to date, can be used to generate the Accrual amount for the current Month.
