Grouping in Reports

All Reports have a Grouping parameter; the options that are available are a combination of Hierarchy Elements and choices appropriate to the Report Type. Five Levels of Grouping can be applied.

The specified  Grouping(s) are used in conjunction with the selected Projects in a nested manner. The lowest Sub Group level is generally where the 'line item' Data from the Projects is reported. Sub-Totals are written out at each Grouping level.

Grouping can be utilized with the Suppress Details option, in which case only the Total lines at every Grouping level are reported.
