Local / Government Tax Modifiers

Description:

Defined at the Local Jurisdiction level, these modifiers will impact the Net Net Revenue result.

May be over-ridden on a per-Asset basis.

Examples:Severance Payments: 8% of all Revenue, 5% of Gas Revenue, 7% of Gas Production

May be truncated when Analysis Case Truncation is applied.

Truncation will take place with the Economic Limit of Net Net Revenue.
