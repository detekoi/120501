OPEX

Fixed OPEX

Each Fixed OPEX entry must be associated against an Asset.

  • The OPEX Entry is initialized with the WI% and POS% of the Asset

An OPEX Entry can be made Dependent on an Expenditure, in which case:

  •  The POS% is calculated as the NPD% of that Expenditure.
  • Updates to that NPD% will propagate to the OPEX item.
  • The Start Date for the OPEX entry must be later than the End Date of that Expenditure.

Variable OPEX

Each Variable OPEX entry must either be associated against a specific Asset, or the All Assets option.

In the latter case:

  • The Variable OPEX entry will be associated with every Asset within the Project.

The Variable OPEX entry must identify a Product, which results in:

  •  The 'matching' (Asset and Product) periodic Production is used to determine the resultant Variable OPEX amount.
  • The POS% and WI% are implicit from the matching Production segment(s).
