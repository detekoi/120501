Escalation

The Escalation page is where you define the percentage rate at which costs will go up over time. This can be applied to CAPEX/Expenditure categories, OPEX and also prices. Resource costs can also be escalated.

The escalation page works by selecting a Jurisdiction and or an economic group from the Escalation hierarchy. Against this selection, an escalation file is applied which means that multiple escalation scenarios can be applied for each Jurisdiction or economic group.

The box under the Escalation Hierarchy allows you to search for the Jurisdiction or economic group you are looking for.

 

In the screen shot above you can see a series of buttons. These allow you to add, delete, rename and duplicate the escalation files.

The columns in the Currency Escalation table are arranged by:

  • Expenditure Categories (A&D through to GEN inclusive)
  • OPEX (All OPEX types)
  • Prices
  • Resource

By typing a number into one of the cells, esi.manage will automatically populate the same number through all subsequent months and years until a new number is entered. Entered numbers appear in bold and automatically populated numbers appear in light grey.  See Currency Escalation Calculations for a more detailed description of how the supplied values are used in calculations.
