Products

Every Production, Reserve and Variable OPEX entry must be associated with a Product. Every Product must be associated with a Product Category.

Reports and the Project Browser can be Filtered based on the Product.

The Bookable flag indicates whether the Production associated with a Product should be used in the Lifting Cost (Dollar / Volume) calculations in the OPEX Reports or Project Metrics.

To Add A Product

  1. Scroll to the last line in the table (it will be empty).
  2. Enter the Product Description (up to 50 characters).
  3. Set the Bookable flag appropriately.
  4. Choose the Category.
  5. Identify whether the Product Quality is represented by Energy.
  6. Provide the conversion to the default Equivalence unit - i.e; 6 mcf of Gas = 0.169044 e3m3 of Gas = 1 boe.
  7. Specify the Quality for the Product, and the corresponding Quality Unit.
  8. Click outside the line to save the change.

 

Changing a Product Description

Once a Product has been associated with a Production, Reserves or Variable OPEX entry, the only options are to:

  • Change the Description
  • Change the Bookable Flag
  • Change the Category

Deleting a Product that is in use is not allowed.
