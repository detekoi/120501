Product Qualities

esi.manage uses J/m3 (Gas) and kg/m3 (Non-Gas) as the internal Quality Units. Your company may require the ability to report in other Quality Units, such as btu, GJ, kg or lb.  

The Product Qualities table is where you will provide the conversion between your companies desired units, and the internal units. When creating a Product, you will need to specify the default Quality in terms of either an internal unit, or a unit defined here.

Production Calculations

Production Units

The entries in the Product Qualities table are used to convert from the fundamental esi.manage unit ratios to some user defined ratio.

WARNING: The Description field is merely text for descriptive purposes only. It plays no role in any calculations.

However, it is best practice to enter text that describes the conversion. For instance, the table claims that 37,447.9 J / m3 = 1 btu / cf. The conversion factor was calculated (and should be calculated) based on data from the Production Units table. This is how the foregoing conversion was calculated:

(1 btu/cf)×(1055.056 J/btu)/(0.02817399 m^3/cf)=37,447.9J/m^3

The J / btu and m3 / cf conversion factors come from the Production Units table. The btu / cf factor was included to make the units work out.

To Add A Product Quality Unit

  1. Scroll to the last line in the table (it will be empty).
  2. Enter the Description (up to 50 characters).
  3. Define the conversion to the appropriate internal Quality Unit.

 

Changing a Product Quality Unit

Once a Product Quality Unit has been used, the only options are to change the Description or change the default conversion.

Note: that changing the conversion will not affect any Production Data that has been entered based on a previous conversion.

Deleting a Product Quality that is in use is not allowed.
