Closing a Budget

 

You can Close a budget by selecting the item off the right click menu in the Budget Editor.

  1. Single left click on the Budget you want to close.
  2. It will become highlighted - now right click it.
  3. Select "Close Budget".
  4. You will be prompted for a confirmation, click "OK"

The original Budget is now closed - all projects will be locked and you will be unable to change any budget details from this point on. This will create a Current Forecast of the Budget that can be used on a go-forward basis.

Creating an Archive follows the same process, you select either "Closed Archive Budget" or "Editable Archive Budget" in place of "Close Budget" in step 3 above. This will not affect the Current Forecast, but will create an (un)editable archive as well.
