Product Categories

Product Categories are used to group the individual Products within your esi.manage database. The purpose of the Categories is in the Product Production report, enabling individual Products within a given Category to be reported in a single unit. Every Product must be associated with a corresponding Category.

 

 

Table Options

 

To Add A Product Category

  1. Scroll to the last line in the table (it will be empty).
  2. Enter the Category Name (up to 50 characters).
  3. Click outside the line to save the change.

 

Changing a Product Category

Once a Product Category has been associated with a Product, the only option is to change the Name.

Deleting a Product Category that is in use is not allowed.
