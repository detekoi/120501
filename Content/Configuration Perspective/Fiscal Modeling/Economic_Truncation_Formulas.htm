Economic Truncation Formulas

This screen allows you to customize the formula used in calculating the economic limit date of a project.

The default “out of the box” truncation is based solely on Operating Income. The economic limit is calculated by finding the maximum cumulative value of the result of the formula.

Items can be added to the formula by pressing the plus icon, or items can be subtracted in the formula by pressing the subtraction icon.

Some items, such as Capital Costs and Operating Costs, allow for additional configuration. Cost types can be selected from a dropdown menu, allowing the included costs to be customized.

Unless a custom economic limit calculation is required, 3esi recommends running the default (Operating Income) method.
